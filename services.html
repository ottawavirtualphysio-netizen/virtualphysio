Service FAQs

Common patient questions

What conditions can virtual physiotherapy support?

Physiotherapy can help with back pain, neck pain, frozen shoulder, arthritis stiffness, sports injuries, post-surgery rehabilitation and balance issues.

How soon should I start post-surgery rehabilitation?

Start when your surgeon or physician clears you. Early rehab often focuses on swelling control, safe motion, walking and gentle strengthening.

Can neck and back pain be assessed virtually?

Care begins with screening and assessment. Your physiotherapist will recommend exercises and education appropriate for your symptoms, medical history and comfort level.

Who benefits from virtual exercise therapy?

Virtual exercise therapy may help patients with painful joints, low tolerance for activity, post-injury weakness or mobility challenges.

Is in-home physiotherapy treatment available?

Yes. In-home treatment in Ottawa is available for patients who prefer physiotherapy care at home. Share your location and preferred time when booking.

Do I need a referral for pain management physiotherapy?

Many patients book directly. If your condition requires medical review, the therapist will guide you toward the right referral pathway.

When to See a Physiotherapist After an Injury

Early assessment can help prevent an injury from becoming more limiting. If pain, weakness, or reduced movement is affecting daily life, a physiotherapy review is often a smart next step.

Getting support early can clarify what is safe, how to manage symptoms, and how to rebuild strength progressively. That approach supports faster and more confident recovery.

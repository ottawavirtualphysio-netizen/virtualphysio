Sports Injury Prevention Tips for Weekend Athletes

Weekend athletes often experience injuries because training is inconsistent and the body is asked to do too much too quickly. Prevention works best when it is simple and sustainable.

A short warm-up, regular mobility work, and careful progression in intensity can make a meaningful difference. If you are returning to sport after a break, a structured plan is especially helpful.

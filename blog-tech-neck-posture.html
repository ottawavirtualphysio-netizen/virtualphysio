How Physiotherapy Helps Tech Neck and Posture Pain

Posture-related discomfort often develops gradually, especially when desk work and screen time are part of daily life. A physiotherapist can help identify the movement patterns and habits contributing to neck tension and headaches.

Small changes in posture, ergonomics, and exercise can reduce strain and improve comfort. The goal is not perfection but sustainable recovery and better movement.

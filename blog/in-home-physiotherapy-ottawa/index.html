In-Home Physiotherapy in Ottawa: Who Benefits Most?

Comfortable, personalized care at home for seniors, post-surgical patients, mobility-limited adults, and anyone who prefers a private recovery setting.

What is in-home physiotherapy?

In-home physiotherapy brings assessment, movement training, education, and exercise guidance directly to your residence. This can be especially helpful when travel is difficult, when you want a private setting, or when recovery is easier in your everyday environment.

At Virtual Physio, care plans are tailored to your needs, your home setup, and your recovery goals. We can support pain relief, mobility, post-surgical recovery, balance, and strength-building with practical, evidence-based treatment.

Who it helps most

  • Seniors and older adults with balance or mobility concerns
  • Post-surgical patients recovering at home
  • People managing chronic pain or limited mobility
  • Families who want private, comfortable care at home

What to expect during your first visit

Your initial appointment usually includes a conversation about your symptoms, a movement assessment, a review of your home environment, and a treatment plan you can use right away. We also discuss the best frequency of visits based on your goals and recovery needs.

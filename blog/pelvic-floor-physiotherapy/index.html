Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy: Symptoms, Benefits and Virtual Support

Pelvic floor physiotherapy can help with bladder concerns, core weakness, prenatal preparation and postpartum recovery in a supportive, private setting.

Common symptoms that may benefit

  • Leakage with coughing, laughing or exercise
  • Pelvic pressure, heaviness or discomfort
  • Core weakness after pregnancy or surgery
  • Diastasis recti and postpartum recovery concerns
  • Pain during intimacy or difficulty with pelvic relaxation

What pelvic floor treatment involves

Treatment begins with a thorough conversation about your symptoms, goals and medical history. Your physiotherapist may guide breathing exercises, pelvic floor activation and relaxation, core coordination drills and lifestyle strategies tailored to your stage of recovery.

Virtual pelvic floor care can be effective for education, exercise prescription and progress monitoring. In-person or in-home visits in Ottawa are also available when hands-on assessment is needed.

Why seek care early

Early guidance helps prevent symptoms from becoming chronic and builds confidence in movement, exercise and daily activities. Many patients feel relief knowing their concerns are common and treatable with the right plan.

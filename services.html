Online physiotherapy services

Physiotherapy services for pain relief, mobility and long-term recovery.

Choose a care pathway below or book an assessment so your physiotherapist can recommend the safest plan. Virtual care is available across Ottawa, with in-home treatment in Ottawa, home clinic room care and person-to-person appointments by request.

In-Home Physiotherapy in Ottawa: Who Benefits Most?

In-home physiotherapy brings evidence-based care into your everyday environment. It is especially helpful when travel, pain, mobility limits, or recovery after surgery make clinic visits difficult.

Patients often benefit when they need a private, practical setting for assessment, education, and guided exercise. Virtual care can also provide a good follow-up option when the main challenge is staying consistent with rehab at home.

Whether you are recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or trying to prevent falls, an in-home program can be tailored to your space and goals.
